Services

Grain Guardians: Cultivating Quality, Preserving Value.

Agricultural Production and Sales Services

Our services cover a wide spectrum of agricultural production and sales activities. We specialize in the cultivation of various crops including Chinese herbal medicine, grains, fruits, vegetables, potatoes, tea, nuts, horticultural products, spice crops, legumes, edible fungi, and sugar crops. Additionally, we provide expert tree planting and management services. Our offerings extend to the sale of livestock, grains, agricultural and sideline products, horticultural products, and real estate Chinese herbal medicines. We also facilitate the purchase and sale of primary agricultural products, ensuring a smooth flow within the agricultural supply chain.

Agricultural Services and Support

We offer comprehensive support services tailored to the agricultural sector. Our services include primary processing of both edible and non-edible agricultural products, ensuring they meet quality standards for consumption or further processing. We also provide essential supplies such as livestock and fishery feeds, feed additives, and feed raw materials, supporting optimal nutrition for animals. Our offerings extend to the sales of fertilizers, agricultural films, and machinery, along with installation, maintenance, and technical support services. We specialize in crop pest and disease control, livestock manure treatment, agricultural machinery services, soil pollution control and remediation, and various technical services aimed at enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability.

Licensed Agricultural Services

Our licensed services encompass activities requiring specific permissions or approvals. These services include animal breeding, grain purchase, harmless treatment of animals, wholesale of pesticides, business of veterinary drugs, business of crop seeds, wholesale, and retail of medicines. We adhere strictly to regulations and approvals from authorities, ensuring legal and ethical practices within the agricultural industry.
